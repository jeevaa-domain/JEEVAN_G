About NPR College

College Details

NPR College of Engineering and Technology is a premier institution committed to providing quality education in engineering and technology.

Established in 2001, the college has grown to become one of the leading institutions in the region with state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty.

College Features

  • Modern infrastructure with well-equipped laboratories
  • Highly qualified and experienced faculty
  • Industry-oriented curriculum
  • Placement assistance and career guidance
  • Research and development opportunities
  • Sports and extracurricular facilities
